01 — About me

The person behind
the architecture.

I'm Ahmed El-Khatib — though most people just call me Khatib.

I started my career as a .NET developer, writing APIs, building backends, and learning how software actually holds together under pressure. Somewhere along the way, Power Platform caught my attention — not as a shortcut, but as a legitimate enterprise platform worth mastering. I leaned in, went deep on Dataverse, Azure integration, and pro-code extensibility, and never looked back.

Today I'm the Power Platform Technical Lead at Trax Group, Saudi Arabia, leading the Power Platform department. That means solution architecture, ALM governance, client delivery, and team direction — across Dynamics 365, Dataverse, Azure, and the full Power Platform stack.

My work lives at the intersection of pro-code precision and platform leverage. I write plugins, build Custom APIs, craft PCF controls, design Power Automate orchestrations, and wire up Azure services — because the right tool matters more than any single paradigm.

I'm available for consultation, conference talks, webinars, and mentoring — because knowledge compounds when it's shared. This blog is where I document what I wish had been written down when I was figuring it out the hard way.
